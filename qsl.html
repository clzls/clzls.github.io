The following is the QSL card style in use. Exchanges of QSL card (either paper, E-mail or eQSL) are welcomed, in any mode including FT8 or through FM repeaters.

  • Paper cards via direct: Post mails and parcels are processed by BY1HT addressed below. Once received I will reply within 1~2 months. No cash, stamp or IRC required. I'm happy collecting stamps and postmarks, and incoming mails will be carefully scaned and stored.
  • Via bureau: The BY bureau only distributes cards once a year, so exchanging cards via BURO may take several years. Better e-mail me first if you would like to exchange.
  • E-mail: bi1mhk*outlook.com (Replace * with @)
  • LoTW, eQSL.cc, QRZ.com: Upload several times a week. No custom style available. (Only when services online, same below)
  • HRDLOG.net (Ham365), hamlog.online (hamlog.ru): Upload once a week or more. Custom style available.
  • clublog.org: Try to upload at least once a month, because of network reasons. OQRS supported but not recommended.
